Our Story

Summit is a hackathon project focused on spaced repetition learning.

Our journey began with a realization. In an attempt to search for the most optimal way of studying in the mountains of work in high school, our founders decided to utilize the spaced repetition method. Paired with our love for nature and the lack of a fully optimized, fleshed out system, Summit came to life.

Purpose

Summit is a spaced repetition learning platform to help students learn more effectively.

Our approach uses algorithms that determine the optimal time to review material, ensuring long-term retention without overwhelming study sessions. This optimization allows for students to learn far more effectively than any conventional flashcard or learning resource. At the same time, we hope to promote care and reverence for our environment and mother nature.
